Sprint Planning

Before Sprint Planning meeting checklist

  • Calculate past performance velocity to help the team forecast.
  • Review the stories at top of product backlog, including Acceptance Criteria and DOD

During Sprint Planning meeting - What checklist

  • Review the past performance
  • The last 3 sprints the average has been X,Y,Z and the average is A.
  • Review the output which is the Sprint Goal.
  • Review the items that will be completed in the sprint.
  • Add stories from the top of the product backlog to the sprint backlog until the total number of points meets the velocity.
  • This assumes that all the stories are ready, if not the team may have to refine a story.

What are Possible Problems for During Planning meeting

  • Backlog items are not fully ready, you will know this prior to going into the meeting.
  • Team cannot agree on Sprint Goal

During Sprint Planning meeting - How checklist

  • Cycle through each PBI
  • Assist the team in determining how it will record the way it is decomposing work for each PBI
  • Listen for blockers or dependencies
  • Ensure participation from everyone
  • Make sure everyone is satisfied with the plan for the PBI
